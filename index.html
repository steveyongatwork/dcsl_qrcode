Make a scannable QR code for any link, with the DCSL wordmark or your own logo in the middle. Download a high-resolution PNG for digital use, or an SVG for print and signage. Everything runs in your browser, nothing is uploaded.

Logo in the middle

Style

Light: a black code on white, the most reliable, scans on any device. Dark: an all-white code on a transparent background for a black or coloured surface. Most modern phones read it, but some older scanners only read dark on light, so always test before printing.

Before you print
  • Use the SVG for signage and print. It stays sharp at any size.
  • The dark background version exports with a transparent background, so it sits on any black or coloured surface.
  • On a sign, minimum width is roughly the scan distance divided by 10. Scanned from a metre away, print it at least 10 cm wide.
  • Always test with a phone before sending to print.
